What is SCC — Spatial Consensus Confidence?
Unlike softmax confidence which only measures what a model predicted, SCC measures where both models looked. Each model independently generates a GradCAM++ heatmap. SCC computes the Intersection over Union (IoU) of these two heatmaps. A high score means both models agree on the same suspicious region — spatially validating the prediction. A low SCC flags the scan for radiologist review, even when softmax confidence appears high.
